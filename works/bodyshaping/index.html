Classes

The goal with every class is to maximize results in the shortest amount of time. In order to do this, we use a combination of CORE, BODY WEIGHT TRAINING and CARDIO VASCULAR EXERCISE. By combining all three of these in a single session, you are able to burn the most amount of fat and strengthen and tone your body. This will help you burn fat even when you’re not working out.

1

CORE

Feel great, feel balanced, feel your natural strength by training and strengthening your core. This will have a positive impact on all your other exercises and the way you feel.

2

BODY WEIGHT TRAINING

Weight training will help you get toned and defined muscles which will not only make you look good, they will assist in burning fat even after the workout is done and you're at home or in the office.

3

CARDIO

To maximize results, we deliberately saved the best for last. Once you've strengthened your core then toned your muscle, we then get into some aerobics that are not only fun but they will keep you wanting more.
